Project Description

As a team of AI enthusiasts, our aspiration during our university journey is to create a remarkable product that leaves a lasting impression. We are driving by the ambition to develop an AI Chatbot that serve as an automated customer support, capable of handling a significant higher workload compared to the current scenario.
